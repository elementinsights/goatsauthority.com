## What is Lyme disease and how is it transmitted to goats?

The bacteria Borrelia burgdorferi, which causes Lyme disease, is spread to animals by the bite of infected ticks, most frequently the deer tick and the Western black-legged tick.

The microorganisms are injected into the circulation of the animal when a tick bites it. Following that, the bacteria start to grow and spread throughout the body.

## Are there any risks associated with feeding dairy to goats?

Dairy products are an excellent source of nutrition for goats, providing them with essential proteins, fats, and carbohydrates. Of course, there are some risks associated with feeding dairy to goats.

Dairy products are also high in lactose, which can cause digestive problems for some goats.

For this reason, you need to introduce dairy products slowly into a goat's diet and monitor them closely for any signs of distress.

## How can you prevent dairy-related problems in goats?
